About Us

PoultryDetect is an AI-powered platform built to help poultry farmers identify and manage common poultry diseases with speed and accuracy. Using advanced image recognition and deep learning models like ResNet, we provide real-time disease detection based on visual symptoms.

Our mission is to promote healthier poultry farming through early disease detection, enabling informed decision-making and reducing livestock losses.
